This study was a replication experiment 2 of the research conducted by Ko et al. in the paper "The Sound of Power - Conveying and Detecting Hierarchical Rank Through Voice", which was published in the 2015 edition of Psychological Science. Of particular interest were the inferences that a listener makes about a speaker's hierarchy by listening to that speaker talk, and whether these hierarchical inferences are in fact impacted by the speaker's hierarchy.
