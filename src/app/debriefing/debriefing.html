This study was a replication of the research conducted by Ko et al. in the paper "The Sound of Power - Conveying and Detecting Hierarchical Rank Through Voice", which was published in the 2015 edition of Psychological Science. Of particular interest were the inferences that a listener made about a speaker's hierarchy from the way that speaker talked, and the relationship between these hierarchical inferences and the speaker's hierarchy.
